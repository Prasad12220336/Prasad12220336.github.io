What is NPTEL?

The National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning (NPTEL) is an initiative by the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India, aimed at enhancing the quality of engineering education in India. NPTEL provides online courses and resources in various disciplines to help students and professionals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality education and learning resources that are accessible to everyone, anytime and anywhere. We aim to foster an environment of learning through our extensive course offerings and innovative teaching methods.

Courses Offered

NPTEL offers a wide range of courses in engineering, science, and humanities. The courses are delivered by professors from premier institutions across India, ensuring that learners receive the best quality content.
